TOURNAMENT RULES

HOW TO PLAY

The player with the most points at the end of the three rounds will be crowned the champion. Don't worry if you miss a round - there are prizes available for the winners of the individual rounds.

TEAM PICKS

Pick the 12 teams you think will finish 2nd in the group stage and the 8 teams you think will qualify as the best 3rd placed teams (4 teams that finish in 3rd place in their group will be eliminated and 8 will proceed to the Round of 32).

For every team you pick who qualifies for the Round of 32 you will win 1 point.

For every team you place correctly you will win an additional 3 points.

The maximum number of points you can win per team is 4.

PLAYER PICKS

You will pick one combo of 2 players from the pre-set list and be assigned a random bonus player at the final selection stage. You will earn 2 points for each goal scored by those 3 players in the group stage (subject to a combined cap of 20 points).

TIEBREAKER

Select the total number of goals you think will be scored in the group stage. This will determine the winner of the round if two or more players are tied on points.

TEAM PICKS

Predict the winner of each match in the last 32, last 16 and quarter finals. Predict whether that team will win the match in normal time, in extra time or on penalties!

You win 2 points for correctly predicting the winner of a match and a bonus point if you correctly predict whether they do it in normal time, in extra time or on penalties.

In the last 16 and quarter finals, you do not need your predicted winner to beat the team that you predicted they would be playing - you will still earn points if they beat a different team.

PLAYER PICKS

You will pick a combo of players from the pre-set list. You will get 2 points for each goal scored by those players in the Round of 32, Round of 16 and Quarter Finals (subject to a combined cap of 16 points).

TIEBREAKER

Select the total number of goals you think will be scored in the Round of 32, Round of 16 and Quarter Finals (not including penalties in shootouts). This will determine the winner of the round if two or more players are tied on points.
