LET'S BE HAVING YOU

Game Rules

PAY TEN OF YOUR FINEST ENGLISH POUNDS TO ENTER.

At the start of each gameweek, pick a team you think will win. You can only pick each team once throughout the competition, except for two pre-selected teams which you may pick twice.

Each week you also pick a player; they score points for goals and assists. Use Haaland wisely.

Teams are split into quartiles based on league position at the start of the gameweek. Points for your selected team's result are awarded based on venue (home/away), result (win/draw/loss) and the quartile gap between your team and their opponent — bravery is rewarded.

Draw points are calculated according to the quartile gap (not flat). The detailed scoring table below lists every permutation for wins, draws and losses by venue and quartile gap.

The competition runs until the end of the season. The player with the most points at the end of the competition wins. What constitutes a gameweek is at the organiser’s discretion and may not align with other fantasy platforms.

BE BRAVE — COWARDICE WILL GET YOU NOWHERE!
